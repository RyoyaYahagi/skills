# HIG Checklist (Summary)

## Navigation
- Choose a primary navigation style and keep it consistent.
- Keep top-level destinations limited and recognizable.
- Provide a clear back path and avoid dead ends.

## Layout and Spacing
- Respect safe areas and device cutouts.
- Align content to a clear grid and use consistent spacing.
- Keep primary actions easy to reach.

## Typography and Readability
- Use platform text styles and support dynamic type.
- Maintain readable line length and hierarchy.

## Controls and Touch Targets
- Prefer standard controls for common actions.
- Ensure touch targets are at least 44x44 pt on iOS.

## Feedback and Status
- Provide immediate feedback for taps and gestures.
- Use progress indicators for long operations.
- Confirm destructive actions and allow undo when possible.

## Data Entry and Validation
- Minimize typing and use appropriate keyboards.
- Validate early and provide clear error messages.

## Accessibility
- Support VoiceOver and clear focus order.
- Maintain sufficient contrast and scalable text.
- Avoid relying on color alone for meaning.

## Privacy and Permissions
- Ask for permissions only when needed.
- Explain why access is required.

## Motion
- Use motion to clarify hierarchy and state changes.
- Respect Reduce Motion settings.
